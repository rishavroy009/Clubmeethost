About

There are so many clubs and societies that it can be tough to figure out which clubs to join. Let’s consider that after dedicated efforts you found some nice clubs and societies for yourself. Let’s say that you have joined only 2 clubs and 2 societies. Each club has it’s normal events, flagship events, meetings, and get-togethers. Moreover, there are always open events, seminars, workshops, etc going on in the college. Okay, finally let me make my point.



How are you going to track this? Will you use the ERP software? You can use it but I’ll not recommend it to you. Why?
